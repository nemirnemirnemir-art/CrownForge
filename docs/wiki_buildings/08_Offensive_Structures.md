# Offensive Structures

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Offensive_Structures)*

---

**Balloon Hangar** - Recruits Balloon Bomber that bombards enemies. Must be watched.
1) Damage dealt by Balloons - Increases damage dealt by Balloons by 100%.
2) Balloons' attack speed - Increases Balloons' attack speed by 50%.

**Cannon** - Fires Cannonballs at enemies.
(see wiki)

**Dovecote** - Releases birds that circle the battlefield and deal 15 damage.
1) Surge of strength - Birds remain on the battlefield longer.
2) Poisoned feed - Bird attacks poison enemies, dealing 10 extra damage over time.

**Firebomb Launcher** - Launches bombs that explode and leave burning ground.
1) Ravenous fire - Increases the spread radius of fire.
2) Damage - Increases damage dealt by 50%.

**Fuel Launcher** - Launches fuel barrels; fuel puddles slow enemies.
1) Increased production - Launches an additional barrel.
2) Lingering oil - Fuel puddle lasts much longer.

**Great Slingshot** - Attacks enemies with a giant stone for 30 base damage.
1) Damage - Increases damage dealt by 75%.
2) Cheaper Production - Ammunition is 50% cheaper to produce.

**Poison Tower** - Attacks enemies with a beam that poisons them, dealing 80 damage over time.
1) Damage - Increases damage dealt by 75%.
2) Fuel decay - Enemies poisoned grant 2 fuel upon death.

**Tesla Tower** - Attacks enemies with jumping lightning strikes for 80 base damage.
1) Attack Speed - Boosts attack speed by 40%.
2) Additional chain - Lightning chains 1 more time.
3) Damage - Increases damage dealt by 40%.

**Timber Launcher** - Launches wooden logs at enemies, dealing 30 base damage.
1) Accelerated logging - Increases sawmills and trees' production by 25% passively.
2) Damage - Increases damage dealt by 100%.
