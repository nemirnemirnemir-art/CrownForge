# Veteran Barracks

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Veteran_Barracks)*

---

**Academy of Fire** - Recruits Fire Mages.
1) Combustion - Fireballs set enemies on fire, dealing 6 bonus damage over time.
2) Damage dealt by Mages - Increases damage dealt by Fire Mages by 50%.
3) Fire Mages' Capacity - Increases capacity by 2.

**Academy of Nature** - Recruits Healer Mages.
1) Healer Mages' capacity - Increases capacity by 1.
2) Healer Mage damage - Increases Healer Mage damage by 25%.

**Barbarian Tent** - Produces Barbarians.
1) Weapon melting - Grants 8 metal upon a Barbarian's death.
2) Cheaper production - Troops are 50% cheaper to produce.
3) Barbarians' damage - Increases Barbarians' damage by 100%.
