# Elite Barracks

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Elite_Barracks)*

---

**Academy of Lightning** - Produces Lightning Mage.
1) Lightning Mages' capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
2) HP and Damage - Increases Lightning Mages' HP and damage by 50%.
3) Jumping Lightning - On hit, lightning chains to 2 nearby enemies with 50% damage reduction per jump.

**Ballista Factory** - Creates Ballistae that shoot from afar and slow enemy advance.
1) Damage and slowness - Increases damage by 25% and applies slow to shot enemies.
2) Ballistae capacity - Increases capacity by 1.
3) Long shot - Projectiles deal more damage the further they fly (up to +100% at max range).

**Black Unicorn Field** - Produces Black Unicorn.
1) Damage - Increases Black Unicorn damage by 100%.
2) Boosters of Morale - +5 morale per Black Unicorn on the field.

**Bottomless Pit** - Produces Hellhead.
(see wiki)

**Cannon Factory** - Produces Cannon (Unit).
(see wiki)

**Catapult Factory** - Produces Catapult.
1) Catapult capacity - Increases capacity by 1.
2) Stun chance - 20% chance to stun enemy on hit for 2 seconds.
3) Long shot - Projectiles deal more damage the further they fly (up to +100% at max range).

**Crystal Castle** - Produces Patricius.
(see wiki)

**Dragon Hatchery** - Produces Flour Dragon.
1) Production speed - Boost production by 70%.
2) HP and attack speed - Increases Flour Dragons' HP and attack speed by 30%.
3) Dragons' capacity - Increases capacity by 2.

**Fiend's Skull** - Produces Bone Fiend.
(see wiki)

**Giant's Bedding** - Free. Produces Giant.
1) Sawdust - +100 Wood when a Giant is produced.
2) Wheat Straws - +100 Wheat when a Giant is produced.

**Goose Hydra Nest** - Produces Goose Hydra.
(see wiki)

**Hydra Pond** - Produces Hydra.
1) Hydras' HP - Increases Hydra HP by 100%.
2) Capacity - Increases capacity by 1.
3) Trait Upgrade - Each Hydra on the battlefield increases damage dealt by all units by 10%, up to 50%.

**Jinn Lamp** - Produces Jinn.
(see wiki)

**Lion Circus** - Produces Griffin.
1) Versatility - Griffins count as all classes (take best bonus from any class). Production cost +100%.

**Pangolin Stump** - Produces Pangolin.
1) Pangolins' HP - Increases Pangolins' HP by 50%.
2) War of Attrition - On hit: -30% movement speed and -30% attack speed for 3 seconds.
3) Pangolins' Evasion - 25% evasion chance.

**Pharaoh's Tomb** - Produces Pharaoh.
(see wiki)

**Pyramid of Horus** - Produces Horus.
(see wiki)

**Ram Pasture** - Transforms Black Sheep into Ram.
1) Rams' HP - Increases Rams' HP by 50%.
2) Spell damage - Each Ram increases spell damage dealt by 20%.
3) Twins - 10% chance of producing an extra Ram.

**Torture Square** - Produces Goliath.
(see wiki)

**White Unicorn Field** - Produces White Unicorn.
1) Unicorns' HP - Increases White Unicorn HP by 100%.
2) Spell Damage - Each White Unicorn on the field increases spell damage dealt by 10%.
