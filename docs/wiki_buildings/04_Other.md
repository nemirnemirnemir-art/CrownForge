# Other

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Other)*

---

**Ancient Pit** - Produces Relics.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Basic Construction** - Can be upgraded into a building of choice after 30s active.
Transforms into one of six Established Production buildings: Wheat Field, Sawmill, Iron Mine, Clay Mine, Vineyard, Crystal Mine.
Runtime note: its selector popup is exclusive across map slots, offers a `Nothing` option, and hides the opener button while the selector is visible.

**Blacksmith's Farm** - Produces ore and wheat.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Boar Feeder** - Places a trough and fills it with fodder to distract wild boars. (Volcano map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Carousel** - Reduces difficulty of Araknor's boss fight. (Graveyard map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Clay Gold Mine** - Produces gold and clay.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Clay Iron Mine** - Produces ore and clay.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Clay Sawmill** - Produces wood and clay.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Dark Academy** - Free. No production.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Fire Brigade** - Extinguishes fires inside your castle. (Volcano map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Gold Iron Mine** - Produces gold and ore.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Gold Sawmill** - Produces wood and gold.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Goldsmith's Farm** - Produces wheat and gold.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Imprisoned Tentacle** - Disrupts the tentacles' protection. (Volcano map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Iron Sawmill** - Produces wood and ore.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Lumberjack's Farm** - Produces wood and wheat.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Phoenix Cannon** - Defends the castle against Phoenix's Fledglings. (Volcano map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Potter's Farm** - Produces wheat and clay.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Pyre** - Creates Pyre spell that defrosts troops. (Graveyard map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Ruin Scouts** - Produces Ruin Scout. (Graveyard map)
Cannot be upgraded.

**Small Peasants' Hut** - Recruits weak peasants.

**Vampire Bar** - Reduces Lord Draven boss difficulty. (Graveyard map)

**Vampire's Mirror** - Produces Dispel Invisibility spell. (Graveyard map)

**Web Remover** - Removes webs. (Volcano map)
