# Basic Production

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Basic_Production)*

Basic Production buildings cannot be upgraded.

---

**Big Well** - Produces water.

**Grape Bushes** - Produces grapes.

**Small Wheat Field** - Produces wheat.

**Starter Clay Mine** - Produces clay.

**Starter Crystal Mine** - Produces crystal.

**Starter Gold Mine** - Produces gold.

**Starter Iron Mine** - Produces ore.

**Tree** - Produces wood.

**Well** - Produces water.
