# Levy Barracks

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Levy_Barracks)*

---

**Anubis Temple** - Summons Anubis' Saber Masters.
1) Damage - Increases damage dealt by Saber Masters by 100%.
2) Trait duration - Increases enrage duration by 5s.
3) Enemy weakness - Weakens 3 enemies for 5s upon its death.

**Archery** - Recruits Crossbowmen.
1) Precise shots - Archers deal double damage once every 5 attacks.
2) Archers' capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
3) Stunning arrows - Archers stun enemies at full HP for 2s.

**Assassins' Temple** - Recruits Assassins.
1) Damage dealt by Assassins - Increases damage dealt by Assassin by 100%.
2) Assassins' HP - Increases Assassin's HP by 200%.
3) Assassins' attack range - Increases assassins' attack range.

**Black Sheep Barn** - Creates Black Sheep that provide Morale.
1) Capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
2) Black Powder - Upon death, Black Sheep explode, dealing 200 damage.
3) Fluff - Each Black Sheep provides +2 extra morale.

**Bone Pit** - Spawns small skeletons.
1) Troop inspiration - Passively increases damage and HP of Undead Troops by 5%.
2) Bone Meal - 90% chance of receiving 5 Flour upon Skeleton's death.

**Cauldron** - Produces Hellpigs.
1) Hellpigs' satisfaction - Provides 10 morale passively.
2) Hellpigs' damage - Increases Hellpigs' damage by 100%.
3) Hellpigs' capacity - Increases capacity by 1.

**Crypt** - Recruits Mummies (Relict).
Cannot be upgraded.

**Fetus Field** - Produces Hellhounds.
1) Juicy Shell - Upon Hellhound's death, you receive 30 water.
2) Cheaper production - Troops are 50% cheaper to produce.
3) Sneak Attack - Hellhounds become invisible at wave start for 20s and get 50% movement speed buff.

**Gnome Dome** - Recruits gnomes.
1) Refund - Upon a Gnome's death, you receive 5 gold.
2) Damage - Increases damage dealt by gnomes by 100%.
3) Capacity - Increases capacity by 5.

**Hunters** - Produces hunters that slow enemies.
1) Stinky nets - Hunters poison enemies, dealing 10 bonus damage over time.
2) Hunters' capacity - Increases capacity by 2.

**Madhouse** - Recruits madmen that use hit-and-run tactics.
1) Alcohol-soaked needles - Madmen make enemies drunk on hit, slowing them down.
2) Madmen's capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
3) Madmen's evasion - 35% chance of evasion.

**Militia Camp** - Recruits Militia/Brigade.
1) Militia HP - Increases militia HP by 50%.
2) Militia capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
3) Mega dude - Produces a MEGA militia after recruiting 4 base troops.

**Nether Portal** - Produces Infernal Brutes.
1) Infernal Brutes' death - Infernal Brutes explode on death, dealing 100 damage to enemies.
2) Infernal Brutes' damage - Increases Infernal Brutes' damage by 80%.
3) Infernal Brute Warriors' damage - Increases Warrior troops' damage by 10%.

**Oasis** - Summons Sobek's Servants.
1) Ashes to ashes - Upon death, Sobek's Servants drop 50 Water.
2) Damage - Increases damage dealt by Sobek's Servants by 100%.
3) Movement Speed - Increases Sobek's Servants movement speed by 50%.

**Peasants' Hut** - Recruits peasants. Higher capacity than starter hut.
1) Capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
2) Insurance - Upon a Peasant's death, you receive Gold 2.
3) Peasants' power - Increases Peasants' HP and damage dealt by 30%.

**Ruin Scouts** - Produces Ruin Scout.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Slingers' Tree** - Recruits Slingers.
1) Slingers' capacity - Increases capacity by 3.
2) Heavy stones - Slingers have 3% chance of stunning an enemy for 1s.
3) Slingers' HP - Increases Slingers' HP by 200%.

**Small Peasants' Hut** - Recruits weak peasants.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Sphinx Guardhouse** - Recruits Sphinx Guardians.
1) Damage - Increases damage dealt by Sphinx's Guardians by 100%.
2) Trait damage - Increases damage dealt upon death by 100.
3) Morale per unit - Each Sphinx's Guardian on the battlefield provides 2 Morale.

**Swordsmen Barracks** - Recruits swordsmen.
1) Damage dealt by Swordsmen - Increases damage dealt by swordsmen by 100%.
2) Swordsmen's Capacity - Increases capacity by 2.

**Whipmen's House** - Recruits Whipmen; they buff allies' attack and move speed.
1) Whipmen's capacity - Increases capacity by 2.
2) Whipmen's HP - Increases Whipmen's HP by 400%.
