# Kingdom Infrastructure

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Kingdom_Infrastructure)*

---

**Archmage's University** - Generates legendary spells.
1) Archmage's University speed - Boosts Spell production by 20%.
2) Illusion of choice - No longer generates random spells but grants spell choice instead.

**Arena** - Provides 25 morale while active.
1) Fight Betting - Earns 1 Gold every 3s while working.
2) Higher morale - Provides additional 15 morale.

**Brick Factory** - Repairs the castle walls.
1) Repair speed - Boosts production by 100%.
2) Fortifications - When castle HP is full, accumulates charges; 5 charges increase max HP by 1 (max 100).

**Buddhist Temple** - Upgrades provide passive effects.
1) Production speed buff - Passively increases all production by 5%.
2) Troop damage buff - Passively increases all damage dealt by troops by 10%.
3) Spell damage buff - Passively increases spell damage dealt by 10%.

**Concert** - Boosts production speed of all buildings under gaze by 30% while active.
1) Music for your soul - Boosts morale by 10 when active.
2) Music for your body - Provides 5 morale passively.

**Dragons' Bakery** - Bakes Flour Dragons into Ash Dragons.
1) Production speed - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Magic scales - Receive 100 crystals after baking a Dragon.

**Execution Ground** - Executes Grunt units and provides Denarii.
1) High bounties - Gain Denarii 2 extra per unit executed.
2) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage and HP of Grunt troops by 10%.

**Fairy Fountain** - Produces random resources.
1) Production Speed - Boosts production speed by 25%.
2) Anti-goblin dust - Starts damaging enemies with each production cycle for 15 damage.

**Furniture Store** - Sells furniture for Town Denarii.
1) Markups - 25% chance of receiving extra Denarii 5 with each production cycle.
2) Skilled bargainers - Boost production by 25%.

**Hero Statue** - Provides troop stat upgrades.
1) Production Speed - Boosts upgrade production by 25%.

**Hospital** - Heals injured troops while active.
1) Masters of healing - Heals 50% more.
2) Masters of morale - Each active hospital provides 5 morale.

**King's Statue** - Speeds up the King's Ability cooldown recovery while active.
1) Crystal Clarity - 25% chance of receiving extra Crystal 1 with each production cycle.
2) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage and HP of Champion troops by 10%.

**Magic Ball** - Increases spell damage dealt by 50% while active.
1) More spell damage - Deals 30% more spell damage.
2) Witchcraft - Provides Arcane troops with a 15% boost to damage dealt.

**Magic College** - Generates basic spells.
1) Magic College speed - Boosts spell production by 20%.
2) Illusion of choice - No longer generates random spells but grants spell choices instead.

**Magic School** - Slowly generates basic spells.
1) Magic School speed - Boost spell production by 25%.
2) Illusion of choice - No longer generates random spells but grants spell choices instead.

**Monument to the King's Gaze** - Adjacent buildings start working without needing to watch them.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Research Laboratory** - Produces blueprints for buildings.
Cannot be upgraded.
Runtime note: its selector popup is exclusive across map slots and hides the slot badge while the selector is visible.

**Research Table** - Produces blueprints for buildings.
Cannot be upgraded.
Runtime note: its selector popup is exclusive across map slots and hides the slot badge while the selector is visible.

**Sacrificial Stone** - Sacrifices Black Sheep for spell reward.
1) Production speed - Boosts production by 25%.

**Tavern** - Reduces wine consumption by 50% and increases morale bonus from wine by 20.
1) Higher Morale - Provides additional 5 morale.

**Tile Enchanter (Epic)** - +35% production speed to a single tile.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Tile Enchanter (Legendary)** - +50% production speed to a single tile.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Tile Enchanter** - +20% production speed to a single tile.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Wheel of Fortune** - Creates a small resource pack.
1) Production speed - Boosts production speed by 25%.

**Worshipper's Sanctuary** - Sacrifices Black Sheep for permanent +1 Morale.
1) Higher morale - Boosts morale obtained from sacrificing the sheep by 1.
2) Production speed - Boosts production by 25%.
