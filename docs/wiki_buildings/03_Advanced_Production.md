# Advanced Production

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Advanced_Production)*

---

**Animal Farm** - Produces meat.
1) Production Speed - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of Rider troops by 10%.

**Carpentry** - Produces Furniture.
1) Neat carpenters - 50% chance of receiving extra Wood 3 with each production cycle.
2) Efficient tools - Boosts production by 25%.

**Fisherman's Hut** - Produces meat.
1) Production Speed - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Quality Lure - 50% chance of receiving extra Meat 2 with each production cycle.

**Forge** - Smelts ore into metal.
1) Efficient Processing - Consumes Ore 2 to produce Metal 2.
2) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of Ranged troops by 10%.

**Fuel Pump** - Produces fuel.
1) Production Speed - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Gifts of the earth - 20% chance of producing a random extra resource.

**Market** - Enables trading of various resources.
1) Faster Trading - Boosts production by 25%.
2) Charismatic Traders - Each market passively provides 5 to morale.
Runtime note: the Market selector is an exclusive overlay popup, supports resetting trade back to `Nothing`, and hides its opener button while the selector is open.

**Mill** - Makes flour out of wheat.
1) Efficient Processing - Consumes Wheat 2 to produce Flour 2.
2) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of Flying troops by 10%.

**Winery** - Makes wine out of grapes.
1) Production Speed - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Wine for All - 50% chance of producing 1 extra wine.
