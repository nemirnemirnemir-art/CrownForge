# Established Production

*Source: [thekingiswatching.wiki.gg](https://thekingiswatching.wiki.gg/wiki/Established_Production)*

---

**Clay Mine** - Produces clay.
1) Zero Waste - 10% chance of repairing your castle for 1 HP with each production cycle.
2) Clay Mine Production - Boosts production by 35%.

**Crystal Mine** - Produces crystals.
1) Magic Aura - Passively increases spell damage dealt by 10%.
2) Crystal Mine Production - Boosts production by 30%.

**Gold Mine** - Produces gold.
1) Rich Veins - 50% chance of receiving extra Gold 2 with each production cycle.
2) Gold Mine Production - Boosts production by 25%.

**Iron Mine** - Produces ore.
1) Troop Inspiration - Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of Warrior troops by 10%.
2) Iron Mine Production - Boosts production by 30%.

**Nether Rune Enchantery** - Produces Nether Rune.
Cannot be upgraded.

**Sawmill** - Produces wood.
1) Sawmill Production - Boosts production by 25%.
2) Friendly Lumberjacks - Provides a 20% boost to the production of neighbouring tiles.

**Vineyard** - Produces grapes.
1) Grape Varieties - Each vineyard passively provides 5 to morale.
2) Vineyard Production - Boosts production by 30%.

**Wheat Field** - Produces wheat.
1) Farm production - Boosts production by 30%.
2) Gold diggers - 25% chance of receiving extra Gold 1 with each production cycle.
